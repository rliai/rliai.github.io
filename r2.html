Grandpa's Lost Legacy

A VR game where the guest follows the fire flies and draws four patterns to recontruct the ruins in a mysterious world.

Project Infomation


Duration 2 weeks (Sept. 19 - Oct. 2, 2018)
Team Size 5 (2 Programmers + 2 Artists + 1 Sound Designer)

Platform HTC Vive
Team members Parker Ramsey, Yanyu (Wren) Tong, Ruoxi (Hana) Li, Linyao (Freya) Li

My Contributions


Programming:

  1. Learned Vive SDK on Unity and implemented code to connect Vive trackers.
  2. Implemented gameplay of drawing on large stones with Unity particle system.
  3. Assisted artists in making environment and setting up models in Unity.

Design:

  1. Took part in designing game mechnism of drawing.
  2. Designed indirect control of using particles to guide naive guests.

Lesson


This is my first VR project and I got familiar with Vive after this round. Also, I learned how to use particle system in Unity and use code to control the particles. This naive guest round taught me the importance of thinking of ways to guide the guests indirectly, which can give them the feeling of freedom. From this, I gradually understood why the open world games like "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" are popular recently and why some levels were designed in those ways. Moreover, I learned how to design play tests and how to iterate through playtesting.
