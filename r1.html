Fruit Drop

Fruit Drop is a AR game where the guest helps the fruit fairy escape from the "WcDonald", using four Buffing/Debuffing fruits as weapons.

Gameplay Video



Project Infomation


Duration 2 weeks (Sept. 4 - Sept. 18, 2018)
Team Size 5 (2 Programmers + 2 Artists + 1 Sound Designer)
Theme Help character A who is afraid of character B
Platform Meta 2
Team members Siyu (Tracy) Chen, Healthy Moeung, Xinyu (Mimi) Wang, Jacky Hong Kei Lee

My Contributions


Programming:

  1. Learned Meta 2 SDK on Unity and implemented gameplay of grabbing objects in AR.
  2. Implemented gameplay of buffing and debuffing.
  3. Assisted artists on making environment and setting up models in Unity.

Design:

  1. Took part in designing game mechnism on buffing/debuffing fruits.

Lesson


This is my first AR project and my first Unity team project in Building Virtual World (BVW) course at ETC CMU. Here I learned some basic functions of Unity such as animator and Navmesh. In this round, our team plan to make a trivial game at first with puzzles, but it worked out not a good choice. So we totally changed our plan in the second week. That is to say, the programming part was finished in one week.
